    "## 3. Z-Score Signal Generation\n",
    "\n",
    "### 3.1 Implement Rolling Z-Score Calculation\n",
    "\n",
    "The Z-score measures how many standard deviations the current spread is from its historical mean. This is the core signal for our mean reversion strategy.\n",
    "\n",
    "**Mathematical Formula:**\n",
    "```\n",
    "Z-score = (spread_t - rolling_mean_t) / rolling_std_t\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "Where:\n",
    "- `spread_t`: Current spread value at time t\n",
    "- `rolling_mean_t`: Mean of spread over lookback window ending at time t  \n",
    "- `rolling_std_t`: Standard deviation of spread over lookback window ending at time t\n",
    "\n",
    "**Trading Logic:**\n",
    "- Z-score > 2.0: Spread is too high → Short spread (Short {SYMBOL2}, Long {SYMBOL1})\n",
    "- Z-score < -2.0: Spread is too low → Long spread (Long {SYMBOL1}, Short {SYMBOL2})\n",
    "- Z-score crosses 0: Spread reverts to mean → Exit position"

    "    \n",
    "    Parameters:\n",
    "    -----------\n",
    "    spread : pd.Series\n",
    "        Spread series to calculate Z-scores for\n",
    "    window : int, default 252\n",
    "        Rolling window size for mean and standard deviation calculation\n",
    "        (252 trading days = 1 year)\n",

    "        Minimum number of observations required to calculate statistics\n",
    "        \n",

    "    print(f\"Calculating rolling Z-scores with window={window}, min_periods={min_periods}...\")\n",
    "    \n",
    "    # Calculate rolling statistics\n",
    "    rolling_mean = spread.rolling(window=window, min_periods=min_periods).mean()\n",
    "    rolling_std = spread.rolling(window=window, min_periods=min_periods).std()\n",

    "    # Calculate Z-score only where we have valid rolling statistics\n",
    "    valid_mask = (rolling_std > 0) & rolling_std.notna()\n",
    "    zscore[valid_mask] = (spread[valid_mask] - rolling_mean[valid_mask]) / rolling_std[valid_mask]\n",
    "    \n",
    "    # Create results DataFrame\n",
    "    results = pd.DataFrame({\n",
